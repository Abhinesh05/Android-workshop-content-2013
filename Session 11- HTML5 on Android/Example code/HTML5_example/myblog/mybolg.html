Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

    Q.1 How can I recover forgot password or account information?

    Sol. Click on the "Can't access your account" link provided at the bottom of Log in window. A form will open asking email address registered with Aakash2. Fill the form and click on the "Get Account Details" button. You will get a link in your registered email account for password change. Click on that link, enter new password & confirm password and click on the "Change Password" button to submit. In case of success a success screen will come otherwise a error message will come. Password changed successfully message will come to your registered email.After that you can login with the new password.

    Q.2 How can I update my account?

    Sol. Login into your account with your existing credentials. Click on "User Information" link given under Users head, present in the left menu-control bar. There you can see your current profile, Click on your name or any of the field, you will find a "Update" button.Click on Update button, a form will open containing your profile information, what ever information you want to update you can update there.After updating information, Click on "Update User" button, your profile information will get updated.

    Q.3 Where should I upload my photo-identity proof?

    Sol. Photo-Identity proof is very important and essential for effective use-Login first, with your existing credentials.After login, you will find "Upload" link on top of every page, click on that link, there you can upload your photo-identity proof.
